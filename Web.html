About The Project

My name is John Doe, a small business owner operating a fruit juice company in Brisbane, Australia.


As consumer behaviour increasingly shifts toward digital platforms, I became interested in understanding how social media marketing affects online engagement, customer trust, and purchasing decisions.


This independent project was developed out of personal interest in digital marketing, retail business growth, and data analytics. The analysis focuses on 2024 consumer behaviour trends among social media users interacting with small retail businesses.


The dataset used in this project was analysed using RStudio and represents consumer survey responses collected in Brisbane, Queensland during 2024.

Project Objectives

Consumer Behaviour

Explore how social media engagement influences purchasing decisions.

Platform Analysis

Identify the most influential social media platforms among consumers.

Trust & Reviews

Examine the role of trust, influencers, and online reviews in purchase intention.

Business Insights

Generate insights that may help small businesses improve online marketing performance.

Key Findings

Instagram Dominance

Instagram appeared as the most influential platform among younger consumers due to its visual and interactive content structure.

Trust Drives Purchases

Consumers with higher trust in online reviews and influencers demonstrated stronger purchase intention.

Short Video Content

Short-form video content generated higher engagement compared to static image-based content.

Mobile Shopping Growth

A large percentage of respondents preferred shopping through mobile devices connected to social media platforms.
